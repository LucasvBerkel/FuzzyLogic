This dataset has been greatly reduced from the original one.
The format of individual reviews has not been changed and our goal is to make our system compatible with the original dataset(with genres that is).
The original dataset contained ~110 million reviews.
The dataset with movies with genres contained ~89 million reviews(~5000 movies did not contain genres)
The dataset with movies with genres and only resent reviews contained ~36 million reviews(Interval was 14-03-2005 till 14-09-2005)
The dataset with movies with genres, resent reviews, only popular movies contained ~33 million reviews(Threshold was 2000 reviews per movie)
The final dataset(without inactive users and mentioned above) contained ~33 million reviews(Threshold was minimum 6 reviews per user)
